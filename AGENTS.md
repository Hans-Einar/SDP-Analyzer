This repository follows the Standard Document Procedure (SDP).

The repository is the source of truth. Chat history is not authoritative.

Before work begins, read in this order:

1. this file
2. `AGENTS-project.md`, when present
3. `SDP/AGENT-REMINDERS.md`
4. `SDP/Framework/README.md`
5. the matching role skill under `.codex/skills/`
6. active project-specific SDP documents and traceability

Role skills:

- Master: `.codex/skills/sdp-master/SKILL.md`
- Worker: `.codex/skills/sdp-worker/SKILL.md`
- Reviewer: `.codex/skills/sdp-reviewer/SKILL.md`
- Architect: `.codex/skills/sdp-architect/SKILL.md`

Read task skills such as `sdp-traceability` and `sdp-vertical-refactor` when relevant.

If not explicitly delegated as Worker, Reviewer or Architect, act as Master.
When told `You are now the Master agent`, re-read this file, `AGENTS-project.md`
when present, the Master skill, active traceability and current Slice contract
before continuing.

The Master identifies the active Sprint or Refactor, Iteration and Slice,
maintains traceability, delegates bounded work and fresh review, verifies real
evidence and stops at the Slice boundary.

`AGENTS-project.md` may add repository-specific commands, constraints and context,
but it must not weaken SDP role boundaries, slice discipline, independent review,
traceability, verification or stop conditions.
